About Me

As an author of fantasy and science fiction, I'm passionate about creating rich, immersive worlds and compelling characters. My writing draws inspiration from diverse mythologies, scientific concepts, and the complex beauty of human relationships.

The Etherfire universe represents my vision of a world where magic and emotion are intrinsically linked, where ancient powers awaken in modern times, and where ordinary people face extraordinary challenges.

My Books

Upcoming Book

The Emberbound Prophecy

When ancient magic awakens, destiny calls...

Sixteen-year-old Aelira Duskbane's life changes forever when she accidentally unleashes Aetherfire—a rare, ancient form of magic—burning down her family's barn. This incident leads her to Stormvale Academy, a hidden school for those gifted in the art of Aethercraft. Within the halls of the mysterious Flameborn House, Aelira struggles to master her volatile powers while being haunted by dreams of a dragon named Emberclaw. Together with the enigmatic Kael Thornhart, she uncovers an ancient prophecy about the Emberbound—a destined soul with the power to either save or destroy their world. But Stormvale holds dark secrets. Built atop a Void Gate, the academy stands as humanity's last defense against an ancient corruption that has already driven the guardian dragons to madness. As enemies emerge both within and beyond the academy's walls, Aelira must make a choice: embrace her connection with Emberclaw and face her destiny, or watch as their world succumbs to darkness.
